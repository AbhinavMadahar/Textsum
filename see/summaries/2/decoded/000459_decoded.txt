fujiyama crest , the legendary racehorse of frankie dettori , has died at the grand old age of 23 .
the stallion became part of racing history when he completed dettori 's ` magnificent seven ' at ascot in 1996 with a victory in the gordon carter h. following that famous win , fujiyama crest became a hurdler for a while before returning to the flat .
he was set to go through the sales ring in 2000 , but dettori purchased his winning horse and fujiyama crest .
