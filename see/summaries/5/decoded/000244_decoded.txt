officers across uk have been condemned for using their powers to stop and search toddlers on suspicion of a host of offences including drug or knife crime .
police said that in nearly all cases , officers had carried out the searches after believing the child had been an innocent party who had been used by adults to conceal drugs , weapons and terrorism .
