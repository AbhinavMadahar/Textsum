british photographer terry o'neill has unveiled a new exhibition of celebrity mothers and daughters , including jo wood , twiggy and judy garland .
the show has been created in homage to the photographer 's own late mother , jo wood , who appear in the exhibition twice thanks to a 1981 shot of the pair on tour with ronnie wood and the rolling stones .
terry has been photographing celebrities for the last 50 years and the new show features both old and new pictures .
