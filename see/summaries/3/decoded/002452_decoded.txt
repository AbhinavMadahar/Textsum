world leaders joined thousands of people to pay their final respects to singapore 's ` founding father ' lee kuan yew .
crowds of singaporeans waving flags braved heavy rain and lined streets to catch a last glimpse of lee as his coffin was taken on a 15km -lrb- nine mile -rrb- procession on top of a gun carriage .
lee , who died aged 91 on monday , ruled for three decades as singapore 's first prime minister .
