russian president vladimir putin has described a secret meeting with officials last year when russia decided it would take crimea .
in a trailer for an upcoming documentary , putin said that he met with security officials in february to make plans for saving ukraine 's then-president viktor yanukovych , who fled power after months of pro-european protests in the ukrainian capital .
` we got ready to get him right out of donetsk by land , by sea or by air , ' he said .
