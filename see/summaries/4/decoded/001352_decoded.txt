aldi , the budget supermarket , is to be official supermarket of team gb as they prepare for the olympic games in brazil in 2016 .
it is understood that aldi will subsidise the costs of training camps and funding community and youth sports schemes with the british olympic association -lrb- boa -rrb- .
aldi 's partnership with team gb is part of a wider strategy to promote the brand as a beacon of healthy , quality , british food .
