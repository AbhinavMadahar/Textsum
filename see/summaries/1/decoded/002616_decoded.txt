northern ireland 's jonathan rea claims back-to-back race victories at the inaugural thailand leg of the world superbike championship on sunday .
rea , on his kawasaki zx-10r , enjoyed a dominant weekend in buriram , firstly taking pole positions and then clinching consecutive lights-to-flag victories on race day .
the wins lifted rea 10 points clear of nearest rival leon haslam at the top of the early-season standings .
